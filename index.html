Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 19, 2026

PorchCast is a baseball scorekeeping app designed for use on your iPhone and iPad. Your privacy is important to us, and we've built PorchCast with a simple principle: your data stays on your device.

Data Collection

PorchCast does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All game data — including teams, rosters, lineups, and play-by-play records — is stored locally on your device and is never sent to any server.

Analytics and Tracking

PorchCast does not use any analytics services, advertising frameworks, or third-party tracking tools. We do not track how you use the app.

Accounts

PorchCast does not require an account, login, or any form of registration.

Third-Party Services

PorchCast does not integrate with any third-party services that collect user data. When you use the share feature to export a PDF box score, the sharing is handled entirely by your device's built-in share sheet.

Children's Privacy

PorchCast does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will post the revised version on this page with an updated effective date.
